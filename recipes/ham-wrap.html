Ham Wrap

Description

A deliciously simple lunch meal full of nutrition.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • 100g of Ham
  • A slice of cheese
  • Peri-mayo sauce
  • Tomato slices
  • Spinach & Rocket
  • Pepper Cracker

Steps

  1. Lay the tortilla down on a cutting board or kitchen bench.
  2. Place some of the peri-mayo sauce onto the tortilla.
  3. Place 3 slices of ham on top of the sauce.
  4. Cut & place tomato slices on top of the ham with a few cracks of pepper.
  5. Place some spinach & rocket on top of the ham & tomato.
  6. Break up a slice of cheese and place on top of the other ingredients.
  7. Wrap the tortilla up & eat!
