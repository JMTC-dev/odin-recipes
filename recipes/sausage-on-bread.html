Sausage on Bread

Description

Simple and brilliant, sausage on bread is something that everyone will enjoy!

Ingredients

  • A pack of sausages
  • Your favourite condiments (e.g. tomato sauce, mustard sauce)
  • White Bread slices

Steps

  1. Grill the sausages on a BBQ or pan until cooked
  2. Optional: Butter the bread
  3. Place the cooked sausage onto a slice of white bread
  4. Top the sausage with your sauce combination
  5. Enjoy sausage on bread!
