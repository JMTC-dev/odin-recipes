Banana Protein Smoothie

Description

Deliciously simple quick and easy hit of protein in the morning, a fresh blend of bananas, cinnamon, oat milk and chocolate protein powder.

Ingredients

  • A bunch of bananas
  • Cinnamon
  • Oat Milk
  • Chocolate Protein Powder

Steps

  1. Pour about a cup of Oat Milk into a blender.
  2. Grab and peel two bananas and place them into the blender with the oat milk.
  3. Shake or sprinkle some cinnamon into the mix.
  4. Add a scoop of chocolate protein powder and blend.
  5. Drink!
